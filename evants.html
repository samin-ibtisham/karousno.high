Sports Festival

It's the day of the Karasuno High School Sports Festival. The volleyball club sends Nishinoya, Tanaka, Kageyama, and Hinata as participants of a four-member relay race. The volleyball club gets super heated while competing with the other sports clubs, with Sugawara warning the others of how their first- and second-years are beyond bad news. True to Sugawara's words, the four prove just how much of a bad news they are.


The day of Karasuno High School Sports Festival has arrived. As part of the festival, an inter-sport club relay showdown is taking place with the winning club receiving a prize of twenty boxes of sports drink mix. The volleyball club sends out their four fastest members as participants: Tanaka, Nishinoya, Kageyama, and Hinata. While Yachi and Kiyoko cheer on their club members, the third-years are worried about leaving the race to their underclassmen. Meanwhile, Sugawara gets competitive with Yoshida, a third-year from the soccer club, in hyping up each others' first- and second-years.
